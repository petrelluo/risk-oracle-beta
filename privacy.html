Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you request early access, create an account, or integrate your brokerage API. This includes your name, email address, and real-time execution data necessary to provide our behavioral risk analysis.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your trading execution data is processed exclusively by our MLOps engine to calculate your risk baseline and flag behavioral anomalies. We do not sell your execution data, trading history, or personal information to third-party data brokers or ad networks.

3. Data Security

We implement enterprise-grade cryptographic security measures to protect your API keys and execution data. However, no electronic transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a resident of California, you have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, request deletion of your data, and opt-out of the sale or sharing of your personal information. To exercise these rights, please contact us at legal@efinancialpal.com.
